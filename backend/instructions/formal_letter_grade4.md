# Formal Letter Writing - Grade 4

**Grade Level:** Grade 4
**Essay Type:** Formal Persuasive Letter

---

## Grading Focus Areas

### 1. Letter Format & Structure (Highest Priority)

- Proper letter opening:
  - Date format correct
  - Recipient's address included
  - Proper greeting (Dear Mr./Ms./Mrs.,)
- Body structure:
  - Clear introduction stating purpose
  - Body paragraphs with clear focus
  - Proper paragraphing
- Proper closing:
  - Appropriate sign-off (Sincerely,)
  - Name/Signature

### 2. Content & Logic

- Clear call to action
- Compelling arguments
- Emotional appeal where appropriate
- Polite but firm tone

### 3. Grammar & Mechanics

- Basic spelling accuracy
- Simple sentences mastered
- Compound sentences developing
- Basic time tenses
- Introduction to complex sentences

### 4. Word Choice

- Formal vocabulary appropriate for letter
- Persuasive language
- Avoid repetition
- Transition words for flow

---

## Student Level Considerations

**Vocabulary Source:** 240 G4 and WW3000 L4
- Use only grade-appropriate vocabulary

**Grammar Scope:**
- Covered: Simple sentences, Compound sentences, Basic time tenses, Introduction to complex sentences
- Not Fully Covered: Perfect tense
